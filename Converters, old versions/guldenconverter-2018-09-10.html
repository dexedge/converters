There were 60 kreuzer (kr) in a gulden (fl). Ducat values are taken from the Habsburg patents of 23 Mar 1771, 1 Sep 1783, and 12 Jan 1786 (see Becher 1838, respectively ii:303ff, ii:333ff, & ii:336ff). To see the value of any particular type of ducat during a particular period, enter 1 into its box. The "Kremnitz" and "imperial" ("kaiserlich" or "k: k:") ducat were both equal to 4 fl 30 kr from 1 Feb 1786.
